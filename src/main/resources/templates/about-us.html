Who We are

Delta Transport Ltd., was founded in 1996 as an International Freight Forwarder.

Delta Shipping Ltd., was founded in 2000 as a Container Transport Operator with a fleet of own trucks and trailers, specialized in container transport worldwide.

Delta Transport Ltd., is the owner of the registered trade mark Delta Group ®.

Fundamental to our success is our belief that forwarding and shipping services depends also about people. We believe that the professional freight forwarder must evolve into being more of an adjunct of any shipping department. With the advent of the electronic commerce, Internet and increasingly sophisticated computerized technology, shipping services are evolving into a partnership cooperation, working for mutual benefit with our customers. We appreciate quality and loyalty of our management and staff and support them by investment in staff training. Only in this way it is possible to offer our clients a good service based on quality.

Delta Group ®, handles all manner of container shipments worldwide with the best possible balance between Cost and Service. The buying power of Delta Group ®, with many reputable shipping lines enables a distinct price advantage to be made available to all our customers. Should you have any export, import or transit cargo, for which you require rates, advice or cargo tracking, we would appreciate the opportunity to assist. The essence of our philosophy is its commitment to giving clients a level of service to match the best you would expect.

As the owner of the first private container terminal in Sofia, Bulgaria we provide 24-hours service for handling, lift-on and lift-off operations of containers, with security personnel. The container yard’s capacity is approximately 800 TEUs.

As a proprietor of our own trucks for inland transportation of containers we are more competitive on the market both concerning lower expenses and strict control over the movement of cargo. The possession of own container trucks and own container terminal gives us independence and possibility to take care for the cargo during the whole transportation process.

Looking forward to providing Integrated Logistics instead of isolated and standardized services, we have built our reputation provides a fast and reliable service, together with customized transport solutions, flexible enough to match the customer’s individual needs.
